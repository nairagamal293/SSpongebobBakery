Our Story Spongebob Bakery

Baking Happiness Since 2010

At Spongebob Bakery, we believe that great baking is equal parts art and science, with a generous helping of love.

Founded in 2010 by master baker Patrick Star, our bakery has grown from a small neighborhood shop to a beloved institution known for our creative treats and commitment to quality. Every day, we craft delicious pastries, breads, and desserts using traditional techniques and the finest ingredients.

Award Winning

Recognized as "Best Bakery" by Bikini Bottom Times for 3 consecutive years.

Premium Ingredients

We source only the highest quality, locally-sourced ingredients for all our products.

Made With Love

Every item is handcrafted with care and attention to detail.
